Are organic products 100% natural?

“Organic” strictly refers to ingredients that are farmed without pesticides, chemicals or artificial fertilizers and it is non-GMO. You can safely assume that an organic product will have between 95-100% chemical free ingredients while for a natural product; this is usually around 50-70%.
